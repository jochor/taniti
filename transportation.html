Travelling to Taniti

Air Travel

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Cruises

Some visitors will arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Small cruises are fantstic ways to explore some of the most beautiful scenery on earth, and allow access to harder to reach ports that major cruise lines cannot reach.
