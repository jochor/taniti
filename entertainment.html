Taniti Entertainment Essentials

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do! We've created a list of some of the most fun things to do while travelling on your vacation to Taniti.Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Visit the local history museum and learn about the island Take a chartered fishing tour Visit one of the local pubs - including a microbrewery! Zip-line through the beautiful rainforest Go snorkeling in crystal-clear water Dance away at the new Dance Club See the skyline with a thrilling helicopter ride Visit the numerous local art galleries
